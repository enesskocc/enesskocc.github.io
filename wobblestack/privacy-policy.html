Privacy Policy — WobbleStack

Last updated: 2026-05-26

This Privacy Policy describes how the WobbleStack mobile game ("the App", "we", "our") handles information. WobbleStack is a hyper-casual physics stacking game published on the Apple App Store.

1. Information We Collect

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA): If you grant tracking permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, Google AdMob receives your IDFA to deliver personalized advertisements. If you decline, ads are still displayed but are non-personalized.
  • Purchase data: When you purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app product, Apple processes the transaction and shares a receipt with our purchase manager (RevenueCat) to validate the entitlement. No payment card information ever reaches us.
  • Local game progress: Your best score, sound preference and unlocked themes are stored locally on your device only (AsyncStorage). These never leave your device.
  • Crash diagnostics: If the App crashes, Apple may share anonymous crash logs with us via App Store Connect. These logs contain no personal information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, microphone or camera data.
  • We do not sell, rent or share data with third parties beyond what is necessary to display ads (AdMob) and process purchases (Apple/RevenueCat).

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — displays advertisements. AdMob privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat — manages in-app purchase entitlements. Privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — processes in-app purchases. Privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. Your Rights

  • Tracking opt-out: You may revoke tracking permission at any time via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Remove ads: Purchasing the one-time "Remove Ads" product disables all advertisements permanently.
  • Data deletion: All locally stored data can be removed by deleting the App from your device.
  • GDPR / CCPA: As we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data to access or delete on our side. For AdMob's data handling, refer to Google's policy linked above.

5. Children's Privacy

WobbleStack is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Ads served by AdMob are configured at a maximum PG rating.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: enesskocc5@gmail.com
