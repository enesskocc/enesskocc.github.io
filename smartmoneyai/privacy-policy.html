Privacy Policy — SmartMoneyAI

Last updated: 2026-05-12

This Privacy Policy describes how the SmartMoneyAI application ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when used with the TikTok Content Posting API to publish video content to a single TikTok creator account owned and operated by the App's operator.

1. Scope

SmartMoneyAI is an internal, single-operator automation tool. It does not provide services to third-party users, does not host user-generated content from outside the operator, and does not expose any public-facing user interface. The App is used solely to publish pre-produced short-form video content from the operator's existing YouTube channel to the operator's own TikTok account.

2. Information We Access

  • TikTok account identifiers: The App receives a creator_info response from TikTok (username, avatar URL, privacy options) prior to each upload. This information is used only to construct a valid post request and is not stored beyond the runtime of a single publishing session.
  • Access token: An OAuth access token issued by TikTok is stored locally as an environment variable on the operator's machine. It is never transmitted to any third party, never logged to disk, and never shared.
  • Publish identifiers: The App stores the publish_id returned by TikTok in a local batch report (JSON) for the operator's own record-keeping. No personal data is contained in these records.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any data about TikTok viewers, followers, commenters, or any third-party users.
  • We do not access TikTok analytics, follower lists, comments, direct messages, or any social graph data.
  • We do not use cookies, fingerprinting, or any tracking technologies.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with advertisers or third parties.

4. AI-Generated Content Disclosure

All video content published through this App is partially or fully AI-generated (narration, script, visuals). Each post includes appropriate disclosure via captions and hashtags (e.g., #ai) in compliance with TikTok's AI content disclosure requirements.

5. Data Retention

Local artifacts (access token, publish IDs) are retained on the operator's machine for the operational lifetime of the project and may be deleted at any time by clearing local environment variables and output files. No remote storage is used.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly process data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the operator via the TikTok account @smartmoneyaiforyou direct message.
