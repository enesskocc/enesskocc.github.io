# Privacy Policy - Roast My NPC

**Last updated: March 9, 2026**

## Overview
Roast My NPC is a mobile quiz game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## Data We Collect
- **No personal data is collected.** We do not require registration or login.
- **No user data is stored on servers.** All game data (scores, settings) is stored locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services

### Advertising
We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers (IDFA with your permission)
- General location data
- App usage data

For more details, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### App Tracking
We request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission to show personalized ads. You can decline this without affecting gameplay.

## Children's Privacy
This app is rated 12+ and is not directed at children under 13.

## Data Deletion
Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to delete. Local game data can be removed by uninstalling the app.
