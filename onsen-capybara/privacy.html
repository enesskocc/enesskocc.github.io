Privacy Policy — Onsen Capybara

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Onsen Capybara ("the App") is a casual game published by Enes Koç. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, or contact details. The following data may be processed:

  • On-device game data: your best score, daily and total capybara counts, and onboarding status are stored locally (AsyncStorage). Never leaves your device; removed when you delete the App.
  • Advertising identifiers & usage data: Google AdMob may collect a device advertising identifier (IDFA), approximate device information, and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays rewarded and interstitial ads through Google AdMob. On iOS, the App asks for your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If you decline, you still see ads, but they are non-personalized. Learn more: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

3. How We Use Information

  • Save your local progress and scores on your device.
  • Display advertising and measure ad performance.
  • Diagnose crashes and improve stability.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Advertising data is processed by Google AdMob under its own privacy policy. We do not operate our own servers or databases for this App.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. Ad content is restricted to a general audience rating (MaxAdContentRating "G").

6. Your Choices

  • Tracking: manage the ATT permission in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Reset local data: delete the App.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with a new "Last updated" date.
