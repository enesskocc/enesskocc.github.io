Onsen Capybara — Support

Welcome! Onsen Capybara is a cozy physics game where you guide capybaras into a warm hot-spring pool.

How to Play

  • Capybaras drop in from the edges of the screen.
  • Drag with your finger to catch and guide them into the pool.
  • Get as many as you can into the water before the round ends!

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I see ads?
The App is free and supported by ads through Google AdMob. Rewarded ads are optional and grant bonus capybaras.

How is my progress saved?
Locally on your device. Deleting the App removes this data.

How do I manage ad tracking?
iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
